## Conflict Resolution

### Code of Conduct Enforcement

**Process**:
1. **Observe**: Notice violation
2. **Document**: Take screenshots/notes
3. **Assess Severity**:
   - Minor: Warning
   - Moderate: Temporary ban
   - Severe: Permanent ban
4. **Act**: Follow CoC enforcement
5. **Document**: Record decision
6. **Communicate**: Inform involved parties

### Handling Disagreements

**When Contributors Disagree**:
1. **Listen**: Understand both perspectives
2. **Facilitate**: Help find common ground
3. **Escalate**: Involve maintainers if needed
4. **Decide**: Make decision if no consensus
5. **Document**: Record decision and reasoning

**Principles**:
- Assume good intent
- Focus on ideas, not people
- Seek understanding before judgment
- Be transparent about decisions
