## Risk Assessment Framework

### Likelihood Scale
- **Very High (5)**: >80% chance of occurring
- **High (4)**: 60-80% chance
- **Medium (3)**: 40-60% chance
- **Low (2)**: 20-40% chance
- **Very Low (1)**: <20% chance

### Impact Scale
- **Critical (5)**: Blocks release, major user harm, data loss, security breach
- **High (4)**: Significant user impact, degrades core functionality
- **Medium (3)**: Moderate user impact, workarounds available
- **Low (2)**: Minor inconvenience, cosmetic issues
- **Very Low (1)**: Negligible impact

### Risk Score
**Risk Score = Likelihood × Impact** (1-25)
- **20-25**: Critical risk - immediate action required
- **15-19**: High risk - priority mitigation
- **10-14**: Medium risk - monitor and plan
- **5-9**: Low risk - accept or monitor
- **1-4**: Very low risk - accept

### Risk Status
- **Open**: Risk identified, no mitigation yet
- **Mitigated**: Mitigation in place, monitoring
- **Accepted**: Risk accepted, no further action
- **Closed**: Risk no longer applicable

## Risk Escalation

### When to Escalate

**Escalate immediately if**:
- Any risk score increases to ≥20 (Critical)
- Privacy/security risk materializes
- Multiple risks occurring simultaneously
- Risk mitigation failing

**Escalate to**:
- Release Manager (first line)
- Engineering Lead (technical risks)
- Executive Team (business risks, delays)

**Escalation Protocol**:
1. Immediate notification (Slack + email)
2. Emergency meeting within 2 hours
3. Decision and action plan within 4 hours
4. Execute mitigation immediately
