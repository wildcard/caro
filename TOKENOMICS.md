# cmdai Tokenomics

## Overview

The **$CMDAI token** is designed to incentivize contributors, reward early investors and maintainers, and ensure long-term sustainability of the cmdai project. This tokenomics model aligns with the open-source nature of the project while creating value for all stakeholders in the ecosystem.

## Token Fundamentals

- **Token Name**: cmdai Token
- **Token Symbol**: $CMDAI
- **Total Supply**: 1,000,000,000 (1 Billion tokens)
- **Token Type**: Utility & Governance Token
- **Blockchain**: Multi-chain (Ethereum, Solana, Base)
- **Emission Model**: Fixed supply with no additional minting

## Vesting Schedules

### Core Contributors (25% - 250M tokens)
- **Cliff Period**: 6 months
- **Vesting Duration**: 24 months linear vesting
- **Release Schedule**: Monthly unlocks after cliff
- **Rationale**: Aligns long-term commitment with project success

### Early Investors (15% - 150M tokens)
- **Cliff Period**: 3 months
- **Vesting Duration**: 18 months linear vesting
- **Release Schedule**: Monthly unlocks after cliff
- **Initial Unlock**: 10% at TGE (Token Generation Event)

### Strategic Partners (10% - 100M tokens)
- **Cliff Period**: 3 months
- **Vesting Duration**: 12-24 months (varies by partnership tier)
- **Performance-Based**: Some allocations tied to integration milestones

### Marketing & Growth (5% - 50M tokens)
- **Vesting Duration**: 36 months
- **Release Schedule**: Quarterly unlocks
- **Usage**: Community initiatives, hackathons, educational programs

## Contributor Rewards Program (15% - 150M tokens)

The Contributor Rewards pool is designed to incentivize ongoing development and community participation.

### Reward Categories

#### 1. Development Contributions (60% of pool - 90M tokens)
- **Feature Implementation**: 1,000 - 50,000 $CMDAI per feature
- **Bug Fixes**: 100 - 10,000 $CMDAI based on severity
- **Code Reviews**: 50 - 500 $CMDAI per quality review
- **Architecture Improvements**: 5,000 - 100,000 $CMDAI

#### 2. Documentation & Education (15% of pool - 22.5M tokens)
- **Documentation Writing**: 500 - 5,000 $CMDAI per document
- **Tutorial Creation**: 1,000 - 10,000 $CMDAI per tutorial
- **Translation Work**: 500 - 5,000 $CMDAI per language
- **Educational Content**: 2,000 - 20,000 $CMDAI for videos/courses

#### 3. Security & Testing (15% of pool - 22.5M tokens)
- **Security Audits**: 10,000 - 100,000 $CMDAI
- **Critical Vulnerabilities**: 5,000 - 50,000 $CMDAI
- **Test Coverage**: 500 - 5,000 $CMDAI per test suite
- **QA Work**: 1,000 - 10,000 $CMDAI

#### 4. Community Building (10% of pool - 15M tokens)
- **Community Moderation**: 500 - 2,000 $CMDAI monthly
- **Support & Assistance**: 100 - 1,000 $CMDAI per issue resolved
- **Event Organization**: 2,000 - 20,000 $CMDAI per event
- **Advocacy & Content**: 500 - 5,000 $CMDAI per piece

### Reward Distribution Mechanism

1. **Contribution Submission**: Contributors submit work via GitHub PRs/issues
2. **Review Process**: Core team and community review contributions
3. **Reward Calculation**: Based on impact, quality, and complexity
4. **Token Distribution**: Weekly/monthly distributions via smart contract
5. **Multipliers**: Long-term contributors receive 1.1x - 2x multipliers

## Community Treasury (20% - 200M tokens)

The Community Treasury is governed by $CMDAI token holders through a DAO structure.

### Treasury Use Cases

- **Ecosystem Grants**: 40% - Supporting projects building with cmdai
- **Research & Development**: 25% - Advanced features, MLX optimization
- **Strategic Acquisitions**: 15% - Acquiring complementary technologies
- **Community Initiatives**: 10% - Hackathons, competitions, awards
- **Emergency Fund**: 10% - Addressing critical issues or opportunities

### Governance Structure

- **Proposal Threshold**: 10,000 $CMDAI to submit proposal
- **Voting Period**: 7 days
- **Quorum**: 5% of circulating supply
- **Approval**: Simple majority (>50%)
- **Execution Delay**: 48 hours after approval

## Risk Mitigation

### Security Measures
- **Multi-sig Treasury**: 5-of-9 signature requirement
- **Time-locks**: 48-hour delay on major changes
- **Audits**: Regular third-party security audits
- **Bug Bounty**: Ongoing program for vulnerability disclosure

### Market Stability
- **Liquidity Provisions**: 8% of supply for market making
- **Gradual Vesting**: Prevents large supply shocks
- **Treasury Management**: Active market stabilization
- **Communication**: Transparent reporting on token metrics

### Regulatory Compliance
- **Legal Review**: Token structure reviewed by legal counsel
- **KYC/AML**: For large allocations and investors
- **Geographic Restrictions**: Compliance with local regulations
