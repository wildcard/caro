## üõ°Ô∏è Brand Guidelines Summary

### DO ‚úì
- Use the ‚ö°üõ°Ô∏è logo combo consistently
- Follow the safety color system (Green/Yellow/Orange/Red)
- Maintain helpful, confident voice
- Use ASCII art for terminal-native design
- Include safety messaging in all communications

### DON'T ‚úó
- Change brand colors or safety levels
- Use condescending or gatekeeping language
- Oversell safety ("100% safe")
- Mix retro aesthetic with modern glossy effects
- Ignore accessibility in color choices
