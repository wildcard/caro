## Testing Your Messaging

Good brand messaging should:
1. **Pass the t-shirt test** - Would you wear it?
2. **Pass the tweet test** - Does it fit in 280 chars?
3. **Pass the elevator test** - Can you say it in 5 seconds?
4. **Pass the grandma test** - Would a non-technical person get it?
5. **Pass the competitor test** - Could this apply to a competitor?

If #5 is "yes", the messaging isn't differentiated enough.
