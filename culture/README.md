## üåü Core Principles (TL;DR)

### Our Mission
*Enable every developer to confidently use AI-powered terminal automation.*

### Our Values
1. **Safety First, Always** - Non-negotiable validation
2. **Radical Transparency** - Open source, open communication
3. **Respect > Ego** - Best idea wins, kindness required
4. **Speed Through Simplicity** - Fast by being efficient
5. **Community Over Company** - Built by and for the community
6. **Excellence with Empathy** - High standards, kind people

### Our Promise
- **To users:** We'll keep your terminals safe
- **To contributors:** We'll respect your time
- **To community:** We'll build this in the open
- **To ourselves:** We'll maintain our values even when it's hard

## ü§î Frequently Asked Questions

### Why do we need culture documents?
Culture doesn't happen by accident. It happens by design. These documents:
- Set clear expectations
- Create accountability
- Attract like-minded people
- Scale our values as we grow

### Who writes these?
Initially the core team, but they evolve with community input. **You** can suggest changes!

### Are these binding?
They're guidelines, not laws. But we take them seriously and expect others to as well.

### What if I disagree with something?
Great! Open a discussion. These improve through respectful debate.

### Do I have to read all of this?
No! Start with what's relevant to you:
- New member? ‚Üí Read the narrative
- Contributing? ‚Üí Read onboarding
- Have questions? ‚Üí Skim and search
