1. Review [launch-checklist.md](/devrel/launch-checklist.md)
2. Complete T-14 and T-7 tasks

**Week 2:**
1. Complete T-3 and T-1 tasks

3. Build community infrastructure gradually
4. Seed early adopters in Discord
5. Create content backlog

1. Use [content-calendar.md](/devrel/content-calendar.md) for weekly rhythm
2. Reference [templates/](/devrel/templates/) for onboarding

4. Iterate based on community feedback
