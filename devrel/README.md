## Quick Start Guide

### For Launch Week

**Day -7 (One week before):**
1. Review [launch-checklist.md](launch-checklist.md)
2. Ensure all items in "T-7 Days" section are complete
3. Schedule social media posts from [social-media-launch-kit/](social-media-launch-kit/)

**Day -1 (Day before launch):**
1. Final review of all T-1 items in checklist
2. Confirm all content is scheduled
3. Team alignment call
4. Get sleep!

**Launch Day:**
1. Follow hour-by-hour schedule in [launch-checklist.md](launch-checklist.md)
2. Post social content as planned
3. Engage, engage, engage
4. Monitor metrics

**Week 1:**
1. Daily team syncs
2. Follow content calendar
3. Respond to all community feedback
4. Track metrics against targets

### For Ongoing Community Management

**Daily:**
- Check Discord and respond to questions
- Monitor GitHub Issues and Discussions
- Post social media content (use content calendar)
- Engage with mentions and community posts

**Weekly:**
- Host office hours (Wednesday)
- Review metrics
- Plan next week's content
- Contributor recognition

**Monthly:**
- Community call (first Tuesday)
- Publish monthly recap
- Review and adjust content strategy
- Plan next month

## Key Principles

All materials follow cmdai's brand principles:

### Voice & Tone
- **Witty, not reckless** - Smart humor, not jokes at users' expense
- **Confident, not arrogant** - We know our stuff, but stay humble
- **Helpful, not condescending** - Educate, don't patronize
- **Technical, not jargon-heavy** - Accurate but accessible

### Brand Consistency
- Use ‚ö°üõ°Ô∏è cmdai logo
- Tagline: "AI-Powered Commands. Human-Level Safety."
- Signature: "Think Fast. Stay Safe."
- Colors: Terminal Green (#00FF41), Deep Space (#0A0E27)

### Community-First
- Authentic over promotional
- Transparent over secretive
- Community input shapes direction
- Recognition for all contributions

### No Buzzwords
Avoid:
- "Revolutionary"
- "Game-changing"
- "Disruptive"
- "Synergy"
- "Leverage" (as a verb)

Use instead:
- Specific benefits
- Real examples
- Honest limitations
- Clear explanations

## FAQ

### Q: Do we have to use all of this?
**A:** No! Use what's helpful, adapt what's not. These are templates, not requirements.

### Q: Can we change the messaging?
**A:** Yes, but stay consistent with brand voice and values. When in doubt, check with core team.

### Q: What if we don't have resources for everything?
**A:** Prioritize:
1. Product stability
2. Core documentation
3. Community channels (Discord, GitHub)
4. Social media presence
5. Content creation

Do less, do it well.

### Q: How do we handle negative feedback?
**A:** See crisis management section in [launch-checklist.md](launch-checklist.md).

TL;DR: Listen, acknowledge, learn, improve. Don't get defensive.

### Q: Can we translate these materials?
**A:** Yes! Please do, and contribute translations back to the project.

### Q: What if launch doesn't go as planned?
**A:** That's normal. Focus on:
- Fixing critical issues
- Listening to feedback
- Iterating quickly
- Being transparent

Progress > Perfection.
