# Caro Landing Page Expansion Strategy

> **Document Version**: 1.0
> **Created**: December 2025
> **Status**: Strategic Planning Document
> **Scope**: 10 specialized landing pages over 12-month campaign

## Executive Summary

This document outlines a comprehensive landing page expansion strategy for Caro, designed to capture distinct audience segments through specialized messaging and targeted conversion paths. The strategy builds on Caro's core value proposition—**safety-first, local-first AI command generation**—while expanding reach into untapped market segments.

**Key Objectives:**
- Increase organic traffic by 400% through SEO-optimized specialized pages
- Improve conversion rates by 3x through audience-specific messaging
- Establish Caro as the category leader for "safe AI CLI tools"
- Build pipeline for enterprise and team-tier sales

- **Privacy is under-messaged** — Most competitors are cloud-first
- **SRE/on-call is underserved** — No competitor targets this niche specifically
- **Open source + commercial** — Few balance community with enterprise
